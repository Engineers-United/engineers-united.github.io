    Engineers United Documentation Site #

    This website is your source for any documentation that is essential for running a successful organization and a successful hackathon.

    Engineers United is both a student organization for supporting UNT engineering students and an organization that puts on the university’s hackathon. That being said let’s talk about the dichotomy between Engineers United and HackUNT.

    Engineers United #

    Engineers United is a student-run, non-profit organization. We strive to bring students together from all backgrounds to solve real-world problems in an innovative way. We aim to have good working relations with all the engineering organizations within UNT because together we can do more than we could alone.

    HackUNT #

    HackUNT is a non-profit hackathon and ran by a community of students from UNT. Our team is made of future software pioneers trying to bring students together on a united front. Our organization has been standing since 2018 and we aim to continue shooting for the stars!

    The documentation structure #

    The documentation is separated between information needed for running Engineers United and information needed for running HackUNT. If you are wondering how to best get involved in this organization checkout the get involved page.

    If you see something, say something! #

    If you see any typos or any information that is inaccurate or no longer accurate please report it by making a github issue on the Documentation repo

    Volunteer Guidelines #

    Pre-Event or Pre-Shift #

    • Familiarize self with Event Action Plan
    • Have knowledge of severe weather safety areas at Discovery Park 1st floor
    • Identify:
      • Fire Extinguisher locations
      • Automated External Defibrillators ( used to treat sudden cardiac arrest)
      • Manual Fire Alarm Pull Stations at exits
      • First Aid Kits

    During Event #

    • Report to your section and let the previous volunteers/organizers know you are there for the shift.
    • Ensure all guests associated with Hackathon have correct credentials ( badge, shirt, etc. )
    • No guests involved with Hackathon are permitted outside of the event area for the Hackathon
    • Perform hourly walk-throughs of Discovery Park 1st and 2nd floor to ensure Hackathon guests, Sponsors, and volunteers are not outside of the permitted event area
    • Inspect Hackathon areas for unsafe conditions:
      • Unsafe usage of power strips
      • Trip Hazards
      • Exits blocked
      • Spills / Slippery Surfaces
    • Keep aisles, walkways, exit paths and entrances clear
    • Be aware of persons with special needs in your area
    • Monitor guests and report undesired behavior to the Event Lead / Lead Organizer
    • Remain Weather Aware: Ensure knowledge of Severe weather shelter areas in Discovery Park )
    • No attendees or persons are allowed to smoke on UNT Campus
